Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a prayer request?
Tap "Submit a Prayer" from the home screen. Write what's on your heart, choose whether to show your name, and tap Submit. Your prayer will be shared with the community for 14 days.
How do I pray for someone?
Tap "Pray for Someone" from the home screen. You'll see a prayer request from the community. Press and hold the Pray button to pray for them. The person will be notified that someone prayed for their request.
Can I stay anonymous?
Yes! When submitting a prayer, toggle off "Show my name" and your prayer will appear as "Anonymous" to others.
Why are some prayers in different languages?
Prayers are automatically translated to your app language so you can pray for people around the world. A disclaimer shows when viewing a translation. Tap "View Original" to see the original text.
What happens after 14 days?
Prayer requests expire after 14 days to keep the feed fresh. You'll receive a notification before expiry with an option to renew if you still need prayer.
How do I mark a prayer as answered?
Go to "My Prayers" and tap on a prayer. You'll see an option to "Mark Answered". This notifies everyone who prayed for you that God answered!

Account Management

  • Delete your account: Open the app > Profile > Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data.
  • Change language: The app follows your device language setting.
  • Notification preferences: Manage in Profile > Settings > Notifications.
