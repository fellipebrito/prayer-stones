Child Safety Standards

Prayer Stones by Bossa Nova Solutions

Effective: March 25, 2026

Prayer Stones, developed and operated by Bossa Nova Solutions and published on Google Play under the developer name Bossa Nova Solutions, is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) of any kind.

1. Prohibition of CSAE

Prayer Stones strictly prohibits all forms of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including but not limited to:

  • Any content that depicts, promotes, glorifies, or facilitates child sexual abuse or exploitation
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind, including AI-generated material
  • Grooming behaviors directed at minors
  • Sextortion or any form of coercion targeting minors
  • Trafficking of minors in any context
  • Any content that sexualizes minors

Users who engage in any of the above activities will have their accounts immediately terminated and will be reported to the appropriate authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC).

2. In-App User Reporting

Prayer Stones provides an in-app mechanism for users to report inappropriate or harmful content, including any content related to CSAE. Users can report prayer requests directly within the app. All reports are reviewed promptly by our team.

3. CSAM Detection and Response

Prayer Stones has established the following procedures for addressing child sexual abuse material (CSAM):

  • All user-reported content flagged as potential CSAM is reviewed immediately
  • Any confirmed CSAM is removed from the platform without delay
  • Confirmed CSAM is reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) via the CyberTipline
  • Accounts associated with CSAM are permanently banned
  • Relevant information is preserved and provided to law enforcement upon request

4. Age Requirements

Prayer Stones is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a user is under 13, their account will be terminated and their data deleted promptly.

5. Compliance with Applicable Laws

Prayer Stones and Bossa Nova Solutions comply with all applicable child safety laws and regulations, including but not limited to:

  • The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • 18 U.S.C. § 2258A (mandatory reporting of CSAM)
  • Applicable state and federal laws regarding the protection of minors

7. Updates to These Standards

These child safety standards may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We are committed to continuously improving our child safety practices.
